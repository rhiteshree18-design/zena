ℹ️ About ZENA

ZENA (Zero-touch ENhanced Assistant) is an AI-powered gesture recognition system built using MediaPipe Hands and JavaScript.

It allows you to interact with your computer naturally — through hand gestures — for actions like zooming, scrolling, and opening websites, all without touching a mouse or keyboard.

Developed as part of an academic project at Atharva College of Engineering to explore touchless accessibility and natural user interfaces.
